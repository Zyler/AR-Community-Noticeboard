Library at The Dock Window Display

107 Victoria Harbour Promenade, Docklands

Emilie Tarp Østensgård communicates a concept to Lotte Schwerdtfeger via a single sheet of A4 paper. Without preconceptions, Lotte Schwerdtfeger interprets this as instructions to create a work.

The collaboration stems from a discourse on the evolution of language, spoken and written, and how error, bias and context can lead to confusion of meaning and intent.

The work aims to investigate slips in translation between mediums, languages and people. Image credit: Illustration by Yoji Matsumura, Photographed Emilie Tarp Østensgård
